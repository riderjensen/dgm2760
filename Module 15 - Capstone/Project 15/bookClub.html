Let Us Help You Decide On A Book To Look For!

"I want a mystery!" (Teusdays 7pm)

This group is for anyone who loves to read mysteries! It's pretty simple, really. Do you crave the a good story? Are you always expecting a twist and get let down when one doesnt show up? Do you dream up all kinds of wild stories that are so crazy that they just might be true if only a cunning villian were behind it? This group will be for you!

As a group, we all vote on a book and usually spend about a month on it. We have dedicated reading schedules but that doesn't mean you cant read ahead! Just dont spoil it for the rest of us, the turn is the best part!

"Show me a romance!" (Wednesdays 7pm)

This group is for anyone who loves to read romance! Are you tired of all the action and violence in books? Are you tired of always having to hear about made up adventures and instead you just want a love story like in the old days? This group will be for you!

As a group, we all vote on a book and usually spend about two months on it. We have dedicated reading schedules but that doesn't mean you cant read ahead! Come join us and discuss why true romance is dead!

"I want to read some sci-fi!" (Thursdays 7pm)

This group is for anyone who loves to read sci-fi! Science Fiction is the best genre in all of writing, not because of the stories but because the fact that maybe these things can happen in our life time. Are you a dreamer of the future? Are you an architect of technology? These stories will provide you with mind food and our group is here to help disgest all of it.

We will choose a book for the group to read and work through it in about a month. Come and discuss all the ins and outs of the future and why Philip K Dick kept dreaming of Electric Sheep!

"I dont know what I want! Give me something random!" (Saturdays 7pm)

This group is for anyone who loves to read - be it drama, suspense, romance, fantasy or whatever else your heart desires! It's pretty simple, really. Do you stay awake past your bedtime to finish that book you just started? Are you 'that' person that tells anyone and everyone about the amazing book you just read in hope that they will get as excited as you and immediately go out and read it so you can talk about it later? This group will be for you!

We will judiciously choose a book for the group to read and schedule a biweekly or monthly gabfest to discuss and gossip about the ins and outs of that book. Depending on the speed of the readers involved, we could have more groups - hey, it's a work in progress! I promise we will have fun!
