Thank you for choosing Cozy Corner Book Repair!

Here are the Cozy Corner, we love your books just as much as you do. but we understand that sometimes, books get a little too much love. Often times, our favorite books can become very worn and/or broken. But they don't have to stay that way. Please use the form down below to enter in your books dimensions and what is wrong with it. We can make you a tentative quote right now and then when you bring it in, we can give you a firm price.

Our simple formula for your books is this: we take the length, width, and height and then get the volume of the book. We then take that value, multiply it by a certain number that helps us to maintain our costs at the bookstore. We then multiply it again based on that factor of what is wrong with it.
